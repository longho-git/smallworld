WELCOME TO SMALL WORLD

The SmallWorld Story

SmallWorld was created in 2010 in North Carolina, America, we are a family-owned business and founded this brand because of our love for children and their creativity. We were motivated to bring clothes that are most comfortable and durable for both outdoor and indoor activities, as well as products that encourage imagination from children. We started small, only having 2-3 people creating and designing the clothes, and now we have stores in over 20 states in the US, thus online stores shipping internationally every year. Our brand has a great commitment to our community and most importantly parents who want more flexible and sustainable clothing choices for their children.

Sustainability

SmallWorld carries one of the most sustainable ranges on the market - our collections include over 99 % products made from sustainable materials such as organic cotton, recycled polyester, and we reuse leftover fabric from earlier collections. For ethical and environmental reasons, we do not use skins, suede, fur, feathers, wool with mulesing or conventional silk in any of our products or collaborations. The choice of material does not only apply to the main fabrics, but also the trims, labeling, and packing of a product. Today, the majority of all these details are organic, recycled or biodegradable. Additionally, we reuse cartons from our suppliers for outgoing shipments to customers, our stores, and retailers.

Diversity and Inclusion

We recognise the importance and power of diversity within our business and, as such, we ensure that our people processes are fair, transparent and promote equality of opportunity for all candidates. It is our pledge that candidates will not be discriminated against on the grounds of gender, gender identity or expression, pregnancy, marital status, age, race, colour, ethnic background, nationality, disability, sexual orientation, religion, religious or similar belief. Every individual will be treated with respect.
